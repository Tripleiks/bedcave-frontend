## 🔧 Frame & Fold: Compact Like a Docker Image

The Vello Bike+ starts with a **chrome-molybdenum steel frame** (or optional titanium at 12.9kg), multisize unisex design with shock absorption and an elastomer magnet folding mechanism. Folded, it's a tiny **57cm x 79cm x 29cm** package – smaller than most server cases, slipping under your desk or into a train like a `docker push` to a registry.[^1^][^4^]

- **Unfolded length**: 155cm – nimble for city streets.
- **Weight**: 13.9kg (without pedals, ±5%), +0.9kg for geared variants.[^1^]
- **Max load**: 125kg – haul your laptop bag and Raspberry Pi cluster no sweat.[^3^]

**Folding time?** Under 5 seconds with patented system – unfold and pedal like `docker run --rm your-container`.[^4^]

> 📝 **Homelab Hack**: Mount a waterproof bag on the optional rear rack for carrying NAS drives. No more bike lock drama in bike-unfriendly cities.

```bash
# Pseudo-Docker analogy for your commute setup
docker run -d \
  --name vello-commute \
  -e GEAR_SHIFT="auto" \
  -p 25kmh:40kmh \
  vello/bike+:latest
```

## 🐳 ZEHUS Drivetrain: Self-Charging Like Kubernetes Autoscaling

The star? **ZEHUS Drivetrain** – a 250W BLDC hub motor with **40Nm torque**, all-in-one Gen2 design (motor, battery, sensors) weighing just **3.2kg**. No frame wires – clean as a Dockerfile with multi-stage builds.[^1^][^2^]

Key geek specs:
- **Speed**: 25km/h assisted, up to 40km/h manual with optional **Schlumpf Speed Drive** (1:1.65 ratio).[^1^][^6^]
- **Range**: 50-75km hybrid/turbo, **theoretically unlimited** via **K.E.R.S. (Kinetic Energy Recovery System)** – pedal backward to regen brake and recharge![^2^][^3^]
- **Battery**: 194Wh integrated (or 173Wh variants) with Smart BMS, 1,000 cycles, 3-hour charge or self-top-up. IPX5 water-resistant.[^1^][^4^]
- **Sensors**: Four smart ones – **tilt/slope sensor** for auto gear shift, 100Hz control loop for responsive performance.[^1^][^5^]
- **Connectivity**: Bluetooth 5.1 app (Zehus/Bitride Connect) for stats, remote control option.[^2^]

💡 **Tip for Devs**: Track rides via app APIs like Prometheus metrics. Integrate with Home Assistant in your homelab for commute analytics – "Battery at 80%? Deploy coffee pod."

This isn't just e-assist; it's **regenerative smart tech**. Backward pedaling flips to recovery mode, recouping energy like Docker garbage collection freeing resources. Perfect for stop-go commutes, extending range without plugging in mid-ride.[^3^][^7^]

## ⚙️ Gates Carbon Belt Drive: Maintenance-Free Like Immutable Containers

Ditch oily chains forever. The **Gates Carbon Belt Drive** is silent, stretch-proof, and lasts 3x longer than chains – no lube, no rust, ideal for homelabbers who hate downtime.[^1^][^5^]

- **Efficiency**: Transfers power like a direct `docker exec` – zero slippage.
- **Optional Gears**: Schlumpf **Mountain Drive** (2.5:1 reduction) for hills, or **Speed Drive** for flats.[^1^]
- **Tires**: 20" Schwalbe Marathon or Kojak for puncture resistance and speed (4.77m-7.15m development).[^1^][^2^]

**Brakes?** Vello hydraulic discs, 160mm rotors – precise stopping power, rain or shine.[^1^]

> ⚠️ **Warning**: Belt requires proper tension (like Docker volume mounts). Check annually, but expect 30,000km+ life.

```yaml
# Vello Drivetrain Config (App-like YAML)
zehus:
  power: 250W
  torque: 40Nm
  kers: enabled  # Self-charging magic
  sensors:
    - auto_shift: slope
    - control: 100Hz
  belt:
    type: gates_carbon
    maintenance: "never"  # Geek heaven
```
